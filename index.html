THE JUGGERNAUT

The juggernaut is an agility hero in DOTA 2. He plays the carry role in the game and has many great abilities to kill enemy heroes. Farming wise, he is great at it. One of his abilities is to summon a healing ward so this helps him greatly to stay in lane. In addition, he his bladestorm ability is very strong early game. Combining that with a support, they can take down almost any hero early. The damage per second is just too high and of great value early game.

As far as pro seen goes, he is still very relevant in today's meta. You'll definitely see the pros pick him. Overally he is just a well rounded hero that gives you a chance to win most games! See below for a list of his abilities!

The Juggernaut Abilties

  • Bladestorm
  • Healing Ward
  • Critical Strike
  • Omnislash
